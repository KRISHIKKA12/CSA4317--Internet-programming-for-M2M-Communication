Founder - Enzo Ferrari

About

Enzo Ferrari founded Scuderia Ferrari in 1929 as a racing team. In 1939, he established Auto Avio Costruzioni, which eventually evolved into Ferrari S.p.A.

due to a contractual agreement with Alfa Romeo. The first car to bear the Ferrari name, the 125 S, was produced in 1947, featuring a 1.5-liter V12 engine.
